About

Welcome to my site! My name is Nathen Watters and I do Web & Mobile Development.

For the past two years I have been involved in QA and Test Automation specifically testing for web UI/UX and web services. I now do web and mobile development bringing in the mindset of a Test Automation developer, and thus practice Test-Driven Development at every turn.

My current pleasures involve both front-end (mobile-first) web development, and back-end web development with Python/Django as well as Node.js/Express.js. You can also find me doing Android application development.
